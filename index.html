About Me

Howdy! My name is Tamir Abu Shaban. I am a student at Texas A&M University pursuing a degree in Management Information Systems at Mays Business School.

This website was created for my ISTM 310 AI Generative HTML Assignment. For this project, I chose to build a professional portfolio that I can include on my resume. I used AI tools to generate and refine the content and structure of this webpage, ensuring a clean and professional layout.

To satisfy the AI media component of the assignment, I explored Google’s Nano Banana for image generation and Google Veo for video creation. I used Nano Banana to experiment with creating professional business-themed visuals, and Veo to test short AI-generated video introductions for potential use in future website versions.
